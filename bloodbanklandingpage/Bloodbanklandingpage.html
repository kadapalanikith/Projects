About BloodBankApp

BloodBankApp is designed to streamline blood inventory management across hospitals. Our app allows hospitals to keep track of blood stock by group and facilitates communication between hospitals to ensure efficient blood transfers.

Key Features:

  • Real-time inventory updates
  • Comprehensive blood group tracking
  • Seamless inter-hospital communication

Key Features

Real-time Inventory Updates

Keep track of blood inventory in real-time, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information.

Comprehensive Blood Group Tracking

Manage and track blood groups efficiently, ensuring the availability of all types.

Inter-Hospital Communication

Facilitate seamless communication between hospitals for efficient blood transfers.

How It Works

Step 1: Register

Hospitals sign up and create an account to start using BloodBankApp.

Step 2: Update Inventory

Hospitals update their blood inventory, ensuring real-time data accuracy.

Step 3: Request & Transfer

Hospitals can request and transfer blood between institutions seamlessly.
